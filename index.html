🌐 What is Globalization?

Globalization is the process that connects countries through trade, technology, communication, and culture.

It allows goods, ideas, and people to move across the world, creating an interconnected global system.

Example: A phone designed in the USA, produced in Asia, and used in Colombia.

🌍 Global Issues

Climate Change

Global warming affects ecosystems, weather, and human life.

Wars & Conflicts

Conflicts create instability and humanitarian crises.

Economic Crisis

Inflation and unemployment affect millions worldwide.

⚖️ Inequality

Inequality refers to the unequal distribution of wealth, opportunities, and resources.

While some people have access to education and healthcare, others struggle to meet basic needs.

This creates social and economic gaps between countries and populations.
