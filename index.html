🌎 World Time

🌍 Globalization

Globalization is the process that connects countries economically, culturally and technologically. It has allowed international trade to grow and ideas to spread quickly. Example: A product designed in the USA, manufactured in China and sold in Colombia.

🎯 Sustainable Development Goals

The SDGs are 17 global goals created by the United Nations to improve the world.
1. No Poverty: End poverty worldwide. Example: financial aid programs.
4. Education: Provide quality education. Example: free schools.
7. Clean Energy: Promote renewable energy like solar panels.
10. Reduced Inequality: Reduce differences between rich and poor countries.
13. Climate Action: Fight climate change with policies.
17. Partnerships: Countries working together globally.

🌍 Countries Progress

Leaders: Finland, Sweden, Denmark → strong education, equality. Challenges: Haiti, Afghanistan → poverty and conflict.

🇺🇸 United States & Moon Mission

The United States, through NASA, is leading space exploration again. In recent years, the Artemis program aims to return humans to the Moon and eventually go to Mars. Importance: - Technological advancement - Global leadership - Scientific discovery
