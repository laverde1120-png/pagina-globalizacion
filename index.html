🌍 Globalization Project

Globalization

Globalization is the process by which countries become interconnected through trade, communication, and technology. It allows ideas, products, and cultures to spread quickly around the world.

Inequality

Global inequality refers to the unequal distribution of wealth, opportunities, and access to resources among people and countries. While some nations have advanced technology and strong economies, others still struggle with poverty and limited access to education and healthcare.

1. No Poverty - End poverty in all its forms everywhere.
2. Zero Hunger - End hunger and improve nutrition.
3. Good Health - Ensure healthy lives for all.
4. Quality Education - Inclusive and equal education.
5. Gender Equality - Equal rights for all genders.
6. Clean Water - Access to water and sanitation.
7. Clean Energy - Affordable renewable energy.
8. Economic Growth - Decent work for everyone.
9. Industry & Innovation - Build resilient infrastructure.
10. Reduced Inequality - Reduce inequality globally.
11. Sustainable Cities - Safe and inclusive cities.
12. Responsible Consumption - Sustainable production.
13. Climate Action - Fight climate change.
14. Life Below Water - Protect oceans.
15. Life on Land - Protect ecosystems.
16. Peace & Justice - Strong institutions.
17. Partnerships - Global cooperation.
