🌐 Globalization

Globalization is the process that connects countries through trade, technology, and culture. It creates an interconnected world where goods, ideas, and people move across borders.

Example: A phone designed in the USA, assembled in China, and used in Colombia.

🌍 Global Issues

Climate Change

A global environmental crisis affecting all countries.

Conflicts

Wars impact economies and societies worldwide.

Economic Instability

Inflation and crises affect millions.

⚖️ Inequality

Inequality means that resources and opportunities are not equally distributed. Some people have access to education, healthcare, and wealth, while others do not.
